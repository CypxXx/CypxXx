Tournament dan leh hrai

1. Team/Squad Pa1 atrang member pa 5 leh substitution pa2 a awm theih ang.Tournament start hma chu team a thlak theih a nga tournament start tawh hnu ah thlak phal a ni tawh lo ang.

2. Knock out ang in kal a ni ang a ,Semi-final best of 3 khelh a ni ang a,Final ah best of 5 a ni ve thung ang.

3. Knock out round ah lobby lut kim hmasa zawk zawk squad in first pick emo an duh duh an thlang thei thung ang.

4. Game chhung ah duh duh sawi theih a ni ang.

5. A hun a khel thei lo emo squad a tel kim thei lo an awm chuan inhriat thiam na nei lo in knock out ang in a kal ang

6. Game chhung a buaina a awm in Team lehlam veve in vawi 2 pause an call thei nga min 2 pause sak an ni ang.

7. Lobby kim hun tur kan sawi a, min 10 thleng nghah theih an ni ang a ,a hnu a lo lut thei lo a nih chuan knock out ang in a kal ang.

8. Kan lo dah belh leh ang :)
